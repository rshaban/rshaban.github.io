What We Do
Bulls Media is a student-run agency dedicated to providing services and equipment to students from all facets of the media landscape, including industry-standard recording hardware and software, DJ lessons, MC-ing for events on campus, and marketing and promotional assistance for USF student organizations.
Our team operates on a simple core mantra: students should be able to get real-world experience working with peers from many different disciplines, and come together to make something truly special. Feel free to stop by our office any time to meet the staff and see where the magic happens! Located on the first floor of the MSC, next to the Bull-Pen.

Podcast Studio
Make your voice heard through a completely self-produced Podcast! All hardware and software provided, as well as links to resources for learning and further instruction. Includes four mics, an iMac, and an audio interface. Seats four people.
Photo Gallery Book Now!
Production Room
With a cutting-edge Mac Pro and industry-leading software, this room is best suited for music production, video editing, and sound engineering! Book in conjunction with our Live Room and record using instruments and microphones in the larger space! Seats one person.
Photo Gallery Book Now!
Live Room
This space offers the highest flexibility possible for recording, rehearsing, and producing music at Bulls Media. Our Live Room is equipped with an upright piano, drum-set, electric keyboard, as well as everything needed to record and produce studio-grade music. Microphones in this room may also be routed into the Production Room for real-time mixing and producing.

DJ Room
This room offers a turn table and speakers for any prospective DJ to hone their skills! Come learn how to DJ from the best USF has to offer with the Bulls Media DJs! Lesson availability varies.
